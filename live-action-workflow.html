LIVE-ACTION COMMERCIALS WORKFLOW

1. BRIEF & PROPOSAL

  • Receive client briefing and clarify needs
  • Define project scope, timelines, and budget
  • Send proposal and align expectations

2. CREATIVE DEVELOPMENT

  • Develop concept and creative strategy
  • Scriptwriting, storyboard, moodboard
  • Present visual and narrative direction

3. PRE-PRODUCTION

  • Cast selection and location scouting
  • Art direction, wardrobe, props
  • Schedule, permits, contracts and tech prep

4. PRODUCTION

  • Filming days with full crew and talent
  • Execute the creative plan on set
  • Capture optional BTS or social content

5. POST-PRODUCTION

  • Edit offline and online versions
  • Color grading, sound design, music/VO
  • Apply motion graphics and VFX (if needed)

6. DELIVERY & LICENSING

  • Export masters (ProRes, H264, social cuts)
  • Deliver via secure link (Dropbox, etc.)
  • Manage licensing rights, backup and renewals
